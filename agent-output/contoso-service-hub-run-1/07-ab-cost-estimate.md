## ✅ Decision Summary

- ✅ Implemented in the validated design: 15 service families across three environments in `swedencentral`.
- ✅ Approved for production economics: AKS, APIM Premium v2, PostgreSQL HA, Front Door Premium, and Azure Managed Redis M100.
- ✅ Savings path preserved: 3-year reservations reduce the monthly baseline from €8,547 to €7,047.
- ⏳ Deferred: multi-region DR, DDoS Network Protection Standard, and customer-managed HSM-backed keys.
- 🔁 Redesign Trigger: if growth reaches or exceeds the 2M transaction target faster than planned, AKS user-pool and PostgreSQL compute scale-up is the first cost inflection point.

**Confidence**: High for the validated baseline | **Expected Variance**: ±10% once live traffic, egress, and log-ingestion patterns are known

## 🧾 What We Are Not Paying For (Yet)

- Multi-region failover stacks and cross-region data replication.
- DDoS Network Protection Standard at the VNet layer.
- HSM-backed Key Vault Premium and customer-managed encryption keys.
- Continuous chaos engineering or dedicated resiliency tooling.
